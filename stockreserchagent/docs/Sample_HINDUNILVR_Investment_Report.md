### **2. Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) Valuation**

This intrinsic valuation model projects future free cash flows and discounts them to their present value.

    *   FY2024 FCF: **₹12,419 Cr** (CFO ₹15,469 Cr - Capex ₹3,050 Cr)
    *   *Source for all CFO data is company Annual Reports. Capex data sourced from various financial data providers compiling Annual Report data.*

*   **DCF Model Inputs & Assumptions:**

    *   **A. Growth Rate (Stage 1: Years 1-5):** **10.0%**
        *   *Justification:* This is a conservative estimate, slightly below the 4-year historical Revenue CAGR of 11.64%. While the company is a market leader, the large base makes high double-digit growth challenging. The Indian FMCG market is projected for steady growth, and HUL's management focuses on volume growth and premiumization.

    *   **B. Growth Rate (Stage 2: Years 6-10):** **8.0%**
        *   *Justification:* Tapering growth as the company matures further and the market becomes more saturated.

    *   **C. Terminal Growth Rate:** **4.0%**
        *   *Justification:* This is a conservative rate in line with India's long-term nominal GDP growth projections, ensuring the company does not outgrow the economy indefinitely.
